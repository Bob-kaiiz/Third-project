ABOUT INDOMIE

Indomie is produced by Indofood, the pioneer of instant noodles in Indonesia and
is one of the largest instant noodles manufacturers in the world. Indomie comes in
many varieties from the classical soup flower such as chicken, vegetable and
chicken cury to our most popular flower Indomie. Available in over 100 countries

Indomie History

Instant noodles were first introduced and marketed in 1958 in Japan.
Due to its pratical and delicious taste instant noodles were well recieved and liked by the
Japanese. Soon, after its popularity spread through out the world including
Indonesia
